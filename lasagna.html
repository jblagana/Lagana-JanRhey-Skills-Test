Lasagna

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese make this lasagna a comforting and hearty dish. Perfect for gatherings and family dinners!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until beef is browned; drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the beef mixture; simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, half of the shredded mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, and egg.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles on top. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
