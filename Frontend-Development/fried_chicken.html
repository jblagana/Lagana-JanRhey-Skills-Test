Fried Chicken

Crispy and flavorful, this fried chicken recipe is a classic comfort food that's perfect for any occasion. Enjoy the golden-brown goodness!

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in chicken pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Dip each chicken piece into buttermilk, then coat with the seasoned flour mixture.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove the fried chicken and drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
